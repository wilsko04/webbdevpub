Do you have the balls to lay your fingers on the precious stone?

The precious stone is the most valuable and most sought after item in the entire universe, excavated from the deep mines of Rongod in the beginning of time.

The prince of Rongod, Maenan is one of a very long line of royalty that have witnessed its true power however nobody except them know anything about it.

Some are very sceptic about the precious stone's actual existence. Some mean that it is all just a fluke, a filthy lie to hide all of Rongod's real problems!

There are even protesters, or as Maenan calls them: Stone-deniers. They are doing everything in their power to expose the royalty about the filthy lie that they belive the precious stone is!

Skeletony has always been aware of the precious stones existence but he was not capable to recruit an army... until now.
